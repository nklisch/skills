# Attestation: How the Open Knowledge Format can improve data sharing (Google Cloud announcement)

## Summary

Google's June 12, 2026 announcement post framing OKF as a vendor-neutral,
agent- and human-friendly standard "formaliz[ing] the LLM-wiki pattern into a
portable, interoperable format." The post positions OKF as standardizing "the
small set of conventions needed to make these patterns interoperable" and ships
three sample bundles (GA4 e-commerce, Stack Overflow, Bitcoin public datasets).
Google Cloud's own Knowledge Catalog was updated to "ingest Open Knowledge
Format and serve it to our agents."

## Key passages

**The positioning (vendor-neutral, formalizes the LLM-wiki pattern):**

> an open specification that formalizes the LLM-wiki pattern into a portable,
> interoperable format. This is a vendor-neutral, agent- and human-friendly
> standard for representing the metadata, context, and curated knowledge that
> modern AI systems need.

**The minimalism framing (small set of conventions):**

> formalizes the small set of conventions needed to make these patterns
> interoperable

**Google Cloud's own consumption (Knowledge Catalog ingest):**

> We have also updated Google Cloud's Knowledge Catalog to be able to ingest
> Open Knowledge Format and serve it to our agents

**Sample bundles as worked instances:** GA4 e-commerce, Stack Overflow, and
Bitcoin public datasets, "produced by the reference agent and committed to the
repo as living examples of conformant OKF."
