Auto detects by content length and viewport: short → slide-over · medium → wide drawer · long or narrow screen → modal.

Why size-detect: a 380px slide-over is fine for a one-paragraph story but cramps a multi-section epic body (and overwhelms a phone). Letting the content + viewport choose means one item-detail surface that's always the right size — small peeks stay lightweight, large bodies get room, mobile always gets the full sheet.
